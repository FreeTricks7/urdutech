Privacy Policy for Mini Player

Thank you for choosing Mini Player. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number when you use the Mini Player app.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information about your device and usage patterns. This includes:

  • Device Information: such as device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • App Usage Information: such as how you interact with the app, the duration of usage, and other related analytics.

2. Permissions

2.1 Media and Storage Permissions

Mini Player requires access to your device’s storage to allow you to:

  • Choose Videos from Storage: Select and play video files stored on your device.
  • Play Next/Previous Video: Automatically play the next or previous video in your media library.

2.2 Network Access

The app needs network access to play video links from the internet.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Enhancing User Experience

We use the information collected to:

  • Improve the performance and features of the app.
  • Provide a seamless video playback experience.

3.2 Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to analyze user interactions with the app. This helps us understand user behavior, improve the app’s functionality, and identify areas for enhancement. Information collected by Google Analytics may include anonymized data such as:

  • Number of users and sessions.
  • Session duration and app usage patterns.
  • Operating system, device models, and other technical data.

3.3 Advertising

We use Facebook Audience Network to serve advertisements within the Mini Player app. Facebook Audience Network may use your device’s advertising identifier to serve personalized ads based on your interests. Please review Facebook's Privacy Policy to learn more about how they collect and use your data.

4. Data Security

We are committed to safeguarding the information we collect. While no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

Mini Player may use third-party services like Google Analytics for Firebase and Facebook Audience Network. These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they manage your information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
