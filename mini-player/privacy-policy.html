Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MiniPlayer (“we”, “our”, or “us”). MiniPlayer is a streaming app designed to play online media, IPTV playlists, and custom network streams with features such as DRM, custom headers, and advanced playback configurations.

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the MiniPlayer app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Optional IPTV/XIPTV Login Data: Some IPTV or XIPTV providers may require a username and password to access their playlists. This data is entered by you and belongs to your service provider. MiniPlayer only stores this data locally on your device within your saved playlist configuration. It is never uploaded or shared with any third party.

The app uses this data solely to make network requests to your IPTV/XIPTV provider to fetch your playlist or stream data.

Playlists and URLs: Any media links, M3U, Xtream, or XIPTV playlists you add are also stored locally on your device. We do not collect or upload your playlists or playback URLs.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

To ensure app stability and improve performance, MiniPlayer may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifiers (via Google AdMob)

All data is anonymized and cannot personally identify you.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality
  • To connect to IPTV/XIPTV servers using locally stored credentials (if required)
  • To analyze usage trends (Firebase Analytics)
  • To display ads (Google AdMob)
  • To detect and fix crashes or performance issues

We never sell, share, or upload your IPTV credentials, playlists, or media data.

4. DRM and Custom Headers

MiniPlayer supports DRM-protected content and custom HTTP headers for secure playback. All such configurations are processed locally and are never transmitted to any MiniPlayer server or third party.

5. Advertising and Analytics

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Opt out of personalized ads: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

5.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage trends. All collected data is anonymous and does not include playlists or login credentials.

Learn more: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All IPTV credentials and playlists are stored locally on your device in private app storage.
  • No data is uploaded to our servers.
  • Uninstalling or clearing data deletes all stored information permanently.
  • MiniPlayer uses HTTPS for secure communication with external servers.

7. Third-Party Services

MiniPlayer may access external IPTV or streaming sources that have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the practices or content of those third-party services.

8. Children’s Privacy

MiniPlayer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Use MiniPlayer without logging in
  • Remove or change IPTV credentials at any time
  • Disable personalized ads in Google settings
  • Clear app data or uninstall to erase all stored data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available within the app or on our Google Play listing.
