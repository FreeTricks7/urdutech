Privacy Policy

MiniPlayer cares about users privacy and, doesn't collect any personal information or data from users or device in any way. Any input or video selected by user for playing from storage stay or saved within your device and are cleared from app when you close, uninstall app or clear the app data. Media Video permission is required for playing next and previous videos from storage, but you can continue without permission or revoke permission any time. If you have any questions, concerns or suggestions you can tell us at:  support@urdutech.xyz
