Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Mini-Player ("we," "our," or "us") is a simple media player that allows users to stream videos from network links and play locally stored videos. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Network Streaming Data

- When you stream videos using a network link, the app processes the URL to fetch and play the video.

- We do not store, collect, or share these links.

b. Storage Access

- If you choose to play videos from your device, the app requires access to your storage to list and play selected files.

- We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal files.

c. Analytics and Advertising

- We may use third-party analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance.

- Our app may display ads, and advertising partners may collect anonymized data to serve relevant ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Support our services through advertising.

4. Data Sharing and Security

- We do not share your personal data with third parties.

- The app does not track your activities beyond analytics required for app functionality.

- We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data processed within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

- If you stream videos from third-party websites, their respective privacy policies apply.

- We are not responsible for the content, tracking, or data practices of external websites.

6. Your Choices and Permissions

- You can grant or revoke storage access permissions at any time in your device settings.

- The app does not require account registration, so no personal information is stored.
