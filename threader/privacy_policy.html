Privacy Policy

1. Collection of Personal Information

Threader does not collect any personal information from users in any way.

2. Permissions

Users need to provide storage read and write permission at the start to import previously downloaded videos and images (if any after clearing or reinstalling the app), and for downloading the latest videos and images to internal storage. However, users can deny this permission at the start or revoke storage permission at any time.

3. Data Storage

Any input or downloaded media details stay or are saved within your device and are wiped out when you close, uninstall the app, or clear the app data.
