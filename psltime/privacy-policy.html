Privacy Policy

PSL Time is a mobile application that provides up-to-date information about the Pakistan Super League (PSL), including match schedules, team squads, and live scores.

Information Collection

PSL Time does not collect any personal information directly from users.

However, the app uses third-party services for analytics and advertising which may collect non-personal data to help improve the app experience and deliver relevant ads.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads
  • Google Firebase / Analytics – for collecting anonymous usage statistics

These services may collect:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App performance and usage statistics

This data is collected anonymously and is not used to personally identify users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any user data. Third-party services may use collected data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
