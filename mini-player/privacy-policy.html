MiniPlayer – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Welcome to MiniPlayer (“we”, “our”, or “us”). MiniPlayer is a streaming application that enables users to play online media, IPTV playlists, and custom network streams with advanced features such as DRM support, custom headers, and flexible playback configurations.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the MiniPlayer app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Optional IPTV/XIPTV Login Data: Some IPTV or XIPTV service providers may require a username and password to access playlists. This information is entered by you and belongs to your service provider. MiniPlayer stores this data only on your device and never uploads or shares it with any third party.

The app uses these credentials solely to authenticate with your IPTV/XIPTV provider and fetch your playlist or stream data.

Playlists and URLs: Any streaming links, M3U files, or Xtream/XIPTV playlist information you add is stored locally. MiniPlayer does not collect, upload, or sync your playlists.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

To improve app performance and user experience, MiniPlayer may collect:

  • Device details (model, Android version)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifiers (via Google AdMob)

This information is anonymized and cannot personally identify you.

2. How We Use Information

  • To enable and improve app features
  • To authenticate with IPTV/XIPTV servers using locally stored credentials
  • To analyze app usage (Firebase Analytics)
  • To show ads (Google AdMob)
  • To enhance overall performance

MiniPlayer does not sell, share, or upload your IPTV credentials, playlists, or media data.

3. DRM and Custom Headers

MiniPlayer supports DRM-protected content and custom HTTP headers for secure playback. All such configurations are handled locally and are never transmitted to MiniPlayer servers.

4. Advertising and Analytics

Google AdMob

MiniPlayer uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads.

More information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Ad preferences: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

Firebase Analytics

MiniPlayer uses Firebase Analytics to understand user behavior and improve the app. All collected data is anonymous and does not include any playlists or personal credentials.

More information: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your IPTV login details and playlists are stored locally in private app storage.
  • No personal data is uploaded to MiniPlayer servers.
  • Deleting app data or uninstalling the app removes all stored information.
  • All network communication uses secure HTTPS.

6. Third-Party Services

MiniPlayer interacts with IPTV and streaming services that operate independently. We are not responsible for content or privacy practices of third-party providers. Please refer to their respective Privacy Policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

MiniPlayer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Use MiniPlayer without logging in
  • Edit or remove IPTV credentials at any time
  • Disable personalized ads in Google settings
  • Clear app data or uninstall the app to erase stored information

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available within the app or on our official pages.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us:

Email: miniplayer.app@gmail.com
